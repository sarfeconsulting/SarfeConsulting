Bookkeeping and Accounting

Bookkeeping is the most basic of all accounting functions. It involves maintaining accurate and updated records of your company's financial activities. Our methodology includes bank reconciliation, purchase and sale records, and regulatory filings. These records are essential to regulatory compliance. Proper bookkeeping makes other accounting functions such as audits, payroll, and tax preparation, much simpler and less time consuming.

Financial Analysis

It is important to conduct periodic review and analysis of your company's financial performance. This includes the preparation of financial statements and reporting for internal and external use with focus on management, corporate and statutory requirements. These reviews are an important part of regulatory compliance and shareholder disclosures. Additionally, financial analysis can provide valuable insight into your company's financial health which can help you make sound decisions for the future.
